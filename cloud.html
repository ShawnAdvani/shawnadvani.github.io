Cloud Distribution




This plot analyses the differences in cloud accumulation across the different latitude lines. Looking at the plot, there seems to be no correlation between the different latitude lines and the cloud accumulation; cloud accumulation is high (100) and low (0) in all of the latitude lines. This would suggest that the cloud coverage is independent of latitude position.
